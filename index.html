OnBoarding

Onboarding is an important process in any emplyoee experience program. it is a critical task, since it has impacts on both compay and its employee. A good onboarding can results in a person who is ready to work effiecently, with confidenc and happy.

On the other hand it will decrease the future cost that the company might face due bad or uncomplete onboarding.

Training

We believe that training and learning is part of any onboarding process. new hirings should adopt themselves to needs of the company and therefore they need to learn some requirements or pre-requisite concepts beforehand. It will help both companies and employees to make sure that after such process, the new hirings are ready to work in the highest capacity they can be in.
